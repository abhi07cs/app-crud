CURL REQUESTS
1. Create a Customer
curl -X POST http://localhost:8080/api/customers -H "Content-Type: application/json" -d '{"firstName":"John","lastName":"Doe","email":"john.doe@example.com","phoneNumber":"1234567890"}'

2. Get All Customers
curl -X GET http://localhost:8080/api/customers

3. Get Customer by ID
curl -X GET http://localhost:8080/api/customers/1

4. Update a Customer
curl -X PUT http://localhost:8080/api/customers/1 -H "Content-Type: application/json" -d '{"firstName":"Jane","lastName":"Smith","email":"jane.smith@example.com","phoneNumber":"9876543210"}'

5. Delete a Customer
curl -X DELETE http://localhost:8080/api/customers/1
